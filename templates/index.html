About RideBuddy

RideBuddy is a carpool coordination tool designed for teams, clubs, and event organizers. It simplifies driver-passenger grouping based on location and activity preferences.

Users can join events, indicate if they can drive, and the app will automatically assign the most optimal routes for drivers.
